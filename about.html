AFYA YAKO CELEBRATION DAY 2022

Afya yako is a non-government organization aimed at helping community to fight against non communicable diseases. The current statistics shows that, most of the young men and women are in danger of having non communicable diseases due to their lifestyles. Afya yako has been in this struggle to help the community.
